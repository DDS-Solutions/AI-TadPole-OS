# Campaign Generation Workflow

1.  **Goal Definition**: User provides a campaign topic (e.g., "Sovereign AI for SME Accountants").
2.  **SEO Research**: SEO Analyst performs keyword research on the topic.
3.  **Content Brief**: Marketing Alpha synthesizes SEO data into a content brief.
4.  **Content Creation**: Content Creator drafts social posts and a blog header.
5.  **Review**: User reviews final brief via Oversight Dashboard.
